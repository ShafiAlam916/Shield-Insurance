## 🚀 Project Overview

**Role:** Data Analyst Intern  
**Company:** AtliQ Technologies  
**Client:** Shield Insurance  
**Domain:** Insurance Analytics  
**Function:** Customer & Revenue Intelligence  

As part of my internship at AtliQ Technologies, I developed a pilot analytics dashboard for Shield Insurance to help stakeholders monitor customer acquisition, revenue generation, policy performance, and demographic trends. The solution transforms insurance data into actionable insights through interactive visualizations, enabling management to identify growth opportunities, optimize sales strategies, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## ❗ Business Problem

Shield Insurance required a centralized reporting solution to:

- Monitor customer and revenue growth efficiently
- Understand customer demographics and purchasing behavior
- Identify high-performing cities and profitable customer segments
- Evaluate the effectiveness of different sales channels
- Analyze policy preferences across age groups
- Improve strategic decision-making using data analytics

The organization lacked a unified analytics platform capable of providing comprehensive visibility into business performance metrics.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

As a Data Analyst Intern, I designed and developed an interactive analytics solution to:

1. Track critical KPIs such as total customers, total revenue, customer growth, and revenue growth.
2. Monitor month-over-month changes across key business metrics.
3. Segment customers into age groups for demographic analysis.
4. Analyze revenue and customer distribution by city and age category.
5. Measure sales channel performance and customer acquisition effectiveness.
6. Understand policy preferences and expected settlement trends.
7. Enable flexible exploration through dynamic filters and drill-down analysis.

The goal was to create a scalable pilot dashboard that provides actionable business intelligence for leadership and operational teams.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 📈 Executive Overview

- Total Customers
- Total Revenue
- Daily Customer Growth
- Daily Revenue Growth
- Month-over-Month Performance Analysis
- Dynamic KPI Monitoring

### 👥 Customer Analytics

- Customer Distribution by Age Group
- Customer Distribution by City
- Monthly Customer Growth Trends
- Customer Demographic Segmentation:
  - 18–24
  - 25–30
  - 31–40
  - 41–50
  - 51–65
  - 65+

### 💰 Revenue Analytics

- Revenue Distribution by Age Group
- Revenue Distribution by City
- Monthly Revenue Growth Trends
- Revenue Performance Monitoring

### 📢 Sales Mode Analytics

- Dedicated Sales Mode Analysis Page
- Customer Share by Sales Channel
- Revenue Contribution by Sales Channel
- Monthly Sales Channel Trends
- Comparative Sales Performance Analysis

### 🎯 Age Group Analytics

- Dedicated Age Group Analysis Page
- Age Group vs Expected Settlement
- Age Group vs Sales Mode Preference
- Age Group vs Policy Preference
- Demographic Behavior Insights

### 🔍 Interactive Analysis

Users can dynamically filter dashboard views using:

- Sales Mode
- Age Group
- City
- Month
- Policy ID
